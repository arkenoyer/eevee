Eevee

The Evolution Pokemon

Pokedex Entry #133

Normal-Type

Height: 1'00" Weight: 14.3 lbs.
First discovered in the Kanto Region.

Eevee is a fluffy brown fox-like Pokemon with light cream coloring on the tip of its bushy tail and the collar of fur around its neck. It has an unstable genetic makeup that allows it to evolve and adapt according to its circumstances. Currently, it is known to evolve into eight different Pokemon:

  • Vaporeon
  • Jolteon
  • Flareon
  • Espeon
  • Umbreon
  • Leafeon
  • Glaceon
  • Sylveon
